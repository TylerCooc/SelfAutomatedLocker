Team Degens




Who we are

We are Team Degens, we are a group of five senior students at California State University, Sacramento. Our group is comprised of four Computer Engineering students and one Electrical Engineering student. Through our combined efforts we are aiming to create a project that can help benefit the health of others.

What our technology does

For our technology we are using a website hosted locker control system. This control system is capable of allowing a user to open and close the locker without physical contact. It has a built in dead-bolt lock preventing thieves. Also, a role system that prevents others from taking your assigned locker as well.

In order to kill bacteria we will be implementing a UV light to clean the locker after a user has unregistered themselves.

Our Goal

Our team is creating a locker that can be used without needing physical contact. With our technology we are aiming to minimize the spread of bacteria in public areas. Also, to allow those who are physically inable to have access to a locker as well.
